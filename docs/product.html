The workflow change

Before

Evidence review happens in spreadsheets and prose. The final claim text can drift away from what the evidence actually licenses.

CAPAS gate

Each claim gets a type, required evidence fields, schema validation, provenance blockers, and a deterministic decision.

After

Reviewers focus on HOLD/REWRITE/REJECT queues, while accepted claims carry explicit audit trails into reports and pipelines.

Pipeline integration

Primary buyer story

For AI governance and scientific data teams, CAPAS answers the question: which candidate claims are safe enough to reuse, publish, or send into model fine-tuning? The economic case is fewer senior-review hours, fewer unlicensed claims in training sets, and a defensible audit trail when downstream users ask why a claim was accepted.

CAPAS gates supplied evidence. It does not infer hidden evidence, certify broad scientific truth, or replace external review.
