# CAPAS — Proof-Carrying Claim Admissibility: Global Case & Sharpened Line

## 1. Thesis

Across every major jurisdiction the same regulatory primitive recurs — evidence must be *re-derivable* from a tamper-evident record, not *declared* in a field a buyer is asked to trust — and the strongest, most budgeted instance of it is pharma GMP data integrity (ALCOA+), where any reported result must trace back to original raw data, where falsification is prosecuted as fraud, and where the EU's draft [GMP Annex 22](https://health.ec.europa.eu/document/download/) explicitly bars LLMs/generative/probabilistic AI from the GMP-critical decision path while permitting only static, deterministic, re-derivable models. CAPAS is the deterministic, itself-validatable gate that admits a claim only when its evidence mechanically re-derives, and issues a portable, independently re-checkable receipt that an inspector, auditor, or counterparty accepts *without re-running it* — sold honestly as gating the deterministic island and only *attesting* (signing, timestamping, provenance-binding) the non-deterministic ocean, and never marketing attestation as verification. The horizontal AI mandates are real but mostly deferred, softened, or voluntary; the defensible wedge is therefore the one black-letter, penalty-backed, LLM-excluded surface — GMP data integrity, EU + US first — expanded laterally on the shared ALCOA+ engine and attached to the cryptographic provenance rails the same regimes are standing up ([eIDAS 2.0 QEAAs](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1183), [C2PA / ISO 22144](https://c2pa.org/)).

## 3. Dialectical conclusion

**Strongest pro.** No competing layer holds all three properties CAPAS needs simultaneously. The deterministic GMP island is the one place where re-derivability is *black-letter* (ALCOA+ "Original/Accurate/Attributable"; "trace any result to original raw data"), where enforcement is *priced today* (60–80% of FDA GMP warning letters cite data integrity; falsification prosecuted as fraud; ASIC and ANPD multi-million fines), and where the entire LLM-verifier class is *legally locked out* of the decision room (EU Annex 22's explicit ban; the symmetric truth that any LLM in an OSFI E-23 / PRA SS1/23 / FINMA decision path is itself an unvalidated model re-triggering the obligation it claims to satisfy). Regulators have begun writing the product spec almost verbatim — the April 2026 [revised US MRM guidance](https://www.occ.gov/) demands "evidence produced as a byproduct of how models are built, not reconstructed after the fact," and MAS AIRM requires an auditor be able to "replicate the implementation… and its results." That is a re-derivation gate, named.

**Strongest con, conceded.** Re-derivation proves *record-to-evidence consistency*, never *evidence-to-reality correspondence* — a faithfully reproduced lie reproduces faithfully (the permanent GIGO residual). The general case is *not* deterministic: GPU float-reduction order, RNG, licensed upstream data, and multi-terabyte frontier training make "re-run it" a fiction, which kills the frontier-lab training-data demand story outright. The regulatory wall is largely *deferred or softened* — Digital Omnibus pushes EU high-risk obligations to 2027–2028, the Court of Rome [annulled the €15m OpenAI Garante fine](https://www.garanteprivacy.it/) (18 Mar 2026), AIDA died, Australia abandoned its guardrails, Japan/Korea carry no real penalties, and US MRM carved GenAI out of scope. And for a single buyer checking a single deterministic pipeline, a 200-line script substitutes for most of the value. Each of these is true and reshapes the company: CAPAS-as-universal-re-derivation-engine is a feature, not a company.

**Synthesis.** The survivable CAPAS is narrower and sharper, and three of the bear case's hits actually *cut for it* once re-aimed. The MRM GenAI carve-out removes only the *non-gateable* demand I already conceded was fake; what remains in scope is the deterministic island that still needs byproduct evidence. C2PA "winning provenance" is the wrong layer — it proves origin/integrity of a *record*, by its own scope "not the truth of the scientific claim," and renders no admit/reject; CAPAS *consumes* it and emits the binding verdict it deliberately omits. And the 200-line script produces a *private boolean*; it cannot cross a trust boundary, because the receipt's entire value is that the verifier was itself validated and the author did not control it. The moat was never the hash compare — it is the **portable, independently re-checkable receipt schema a non-trusting third party accepts as dispositive**, plus the one-time IQ/OQ/PQ validation tax that, once paid, locks out every later entrant and the entire LLM class symmetrically.

**The reconciled position in one breath.** The defensible CAPAS is the *itself-validated gate + trust-transfer receipt* for the *deterministic island where re-derivability is already black-letter and LLMs are legally barred* — pharma GMP data integrity, EU/US first — expanding laterally on one ALCOA+ engine across UK/CA/AU/CH/JP/KR/IN/BR/SG, attaching to the cryptographic provenance rails (eIDAS QEAA, C2PA/ISO 22144) the same regimes mandate, honest about *gating* the island and only *attesting* the ocean. It occupies a decision-boundary room that LLM-judges are locked out of and that packagers and scripts structurally cannot enter.

## 4. Product implications

**The verification ladder** (increasing assurance, increasing cost/feasibility constraint):

1. **Form** — claim is well-formed, fields present, schema-valid. Cheap, commodity, near-zero defensibility on its own.
2. **Claim-evidence alignment** — the asserted result is consistent with the submitted evidence (recalculation, range/audit-trail completeness, ALCOA+ "Complete/Consistent"). The first rung with real value.
3. **Re-execution** — the result mechanically re-derives from declared inputs in a deterministic environment (fixed-seed/containerized analyses, chromatography raw-to-result re-integration, batch-release calculations, deterministic inference on fixed weights). **This is the gate.**
4. **ZK proof** — re-derivability demonstrated without exposing protected inputs (licensed data, PHI, IP). Strategically important for the "evidence cannot be re-shipped" cases; later rung.
5. **Signed provenance** — the verdict and its substrate bound into a portable, tamper-evident, independently re-checkable receipt ([eIDAS QEAA](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1183), [C2PA / ISO 22144](https://c2pa.org/) manifest) an inspector/auditor/counterparty accepts without re-running. **This is the moat.**

**Build first (the three highest-leverage things):**

1. **Rung 3 for the GMP island + its validation package.** The deterministic re-execution gate for chromatography/instrument data integrity, *shipped with* its IQ/OQ/PQ documentation, GAMP 5 categorization, and CSV dossier. The validation package is a product artifact, not an afterthought — it is the entry key that lets CAPAS touch a GxP record at all, and once paid it is the moat that re-triggers revalidation on any rip-out. This directly neutralizes the "validate-the-validator" paradox.
2. **Rung 5: the receipt schema + independent re-checker, bound to a real cryptographic substrate.** The only thing the 200-line script structurally cannot replicate: an artifact that transfers trust across a boundary. Build the verifier-side re-checker first (the side that creates inspector/auditor pull) to seed the two-sided standard — this is the genuine network effect.
3. **The scope-partition engine.** An automatic deterministic-vs-nondeterministic classifier that routes each claim to **GATE** (re-derive, rung 3) or **ATTEST** (sign/bind/timestamp only, rung 5 without rung 3) and labels the receipt accordingly. This is what makes CAPAS *legally safe to sell* — never overclaiming verification on a nondeterministic artifact — and converts the GIGO and nondeterminism objections into a product feature.

**The honest scope.**
- *Fully gateable now (re-execute):* analytical-instrument raw-to-result re-integration and audit-trail completeness; fixed-seed/containerized statistical pipelines, batch-release and LIMS-derived results, dossier numerical claims; hash/signature/provenance-chain integrity over packaged artifacts (RO-Crate/in-toto/C2PA consumption); deterministic ML inference on fixed weights + fixed input.
- *Attestation-only (sign/timestamp/bind — never marketed as verification):* any GPU-nondeterministic or frontier training run; claims depending on licensed/NDA upstream data that cannot be re-shipped; wall-clock/external-API/live-data-dependent processes; and the **truth** of the underlying measurement (the GIGO residual, attestation-only by construction).
- *The rule:* CAPAS **gates the deterministic island and attests everything else, and never conflates the two** — conflating them is what gets a compliance vendor sued.
- *Explicitly deprioritized (the bear case wins):* frontier-lab training-data re-derivation (non-deterministic); publishers (no funded buyer); China (state-registry + source-disclosure bars a foreign gate); generic finance MRM as *entry* (incumbent-owned, principles-based — cross-sell only once the receipt schema is proven).

## 5. The defensible one-line claim

> **CAPAS is the deterministic, itself-validatable gate that admits a claim only when its evidence mechanically re-derives — and issues a portable, re-checkable receipt that an inspector, auditor, or counterparty accepts without re-running it; for the regulated, deterministic processes (starting with pharma GMP data integrity) where re-derivability is already the legal standard and non-deterministic LLM verifiers are barred from the decision path.**

It does *not* claim to verify truth (GIGO), to re-derive frontier training (non-deterministic), or to be required everywhere (black-letter only in GMP today). Every word survives the bear case.
