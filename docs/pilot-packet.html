Two-week deployment packet

Stand up a governed evidence-gating workflow without pretending the browser certifies truth.

CAPAS packages claim typing, deterministic gating, human confirmation, batch reporting, and provenance blockers into a pilot that a technical team can measure and a steering committee can understand.

Delivery path

1. ScopeSelect corpus, owner, reviewer, and baseline review process.
2. IngestBring paper text, metadata exports, theorem notes, or structured records.
3. ConfirmHuman reviewer approves candidate spans before CAPAS decides.
4. GateRun single/batch CAPAS decisions with schema v3 evidence contracts.
5. Read outDeliver decision mix, exception queue, provenance blockers, and ROI model.

Buyer-ready artifacts

Audit export

CSV/JSON with verdict, reason, schema version, record ID, evidence fields, and provenance blockers.

Exception queue

HOLD/REWRITE/REJECT records routed for expert adjudication and wording repair.

Capacity model

Baseline review minutes compared with CAPAS-guided triage minutes and measured pilot deltas.

Required footnotes

  • CAPAS outputs are deterministic gates over supplied evidence fields, not LLM judgments.
  • Fine-tune readiness requires CLI/API provenance checks for source hashes, RO-Crate packets, witness registries, review hashes, and reviewer attestations.
  • All third-party source materials remain subject to their original license, confidentiality, copyright, and database-right constraints.
  • Stress-test and planning metrics are not customer production claims unless separately measured and approved.
